House Prices Dataset:
  1. Common years were chosen between House Prices Dataset and Number of Residential Sales Dataset, 1995-2022.
  2. Then only the spreadsheet "Table 11", was chosen, and only the attribute "All Dwellings Price" was kept, rest were discarded.
  3. Since the timescales were different, processing was done to bring them onto a common ground.
  4. Took the average over the four quarters of the year and used that as entries on a per year basis.
  5. Each region was processed and stored in the JSON files.
Number of Residential Sales in the UK Dataset:
  1. Common years were chosen between House Prices Dataset and Number of Residential Sales Dataset, 1995-2022.
  2. Then only the spreadsheet "1a", was chosen.
  3. Since the timescales were different, processing was done to bring them onto a common ground.
  4. Took the value of the fourth quarter only, because the timescale was on a year-end basis and thus, the fourth quarter would cover the whole year. Thus used those as entries on a per year basis.
  5. Each region was processed and stored in the JSON files.
Combined Dataset:
Only the common regions were loaded, and any inconsistencies in the region names were handled accordingly.
